Let's Feed Jay!

You will be shown a number of cards. Each card you click on will reveal an adorable picture of Jay. You need to find the other card with the matching picture of Jay.

For every pair of cards you click on that is a match, you add 1 pebble of food to Jay's dinner. For every pair that is not a match, you take away 1 pebble from his dinner.

Your goal is to make sure Jay has enough to eat to make it through the night.

Choose a level:

Difficulty: EASY

Jay needs at least 1 pebble to not keel over and bite the dust.
Difficulty: MEDIUM

Jay needs at least 3 pebbles to not keel over and bite the dust.
Difficulty: HARD

Jay needs at least 5 pebbles to not keel over and bite the dust.
Pebble Count: 0
